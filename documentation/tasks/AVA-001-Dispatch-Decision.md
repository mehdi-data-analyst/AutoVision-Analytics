# Business Problem

The operations team needs a simple decision system to determine whether a dispatch request should be approved based on predefined business rules.

---

# Objective

Build a Python program that evaluates dispatch conditions and returns an approval decision.

---

# Business Rules

- Short distance requests can be completed without special transportation.
- Medium distance requests require a bicycle and suitable weather.
- Long distance requests require either a company car or a ride service.
- Invalid requests are rejected.
